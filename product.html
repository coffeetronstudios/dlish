WHY ORGANIC?
Organic products are not just boutique characteristics of high end food and agricultural products, they offer much, much more. We initially made these confections for our family and didn't want foods laced with any unknown combination of funigicdes, pesticides, herbicides, hormones, or antibiotics, and we didn't think you'd want to either. Here are our top 5 reasons:
1. Reduce persistent chemicals and pesticides.
Buying organic food promotes a less toxic environment for all living things.
2. Avoid rBGH, GMOs, sub-therapeautic anti-bodies, and cloned food.
These food technologies have been rushed to the market with little to no long-term testing. Should our children be the guinea pigs for these tests? Most European counties ban most of these practices outright.
3. Reduce, if not eliminate, off-farm pollution.
The chemicals used to grow our crops and feed our cattle don't just go away. They flow downstream. Genetically modified seeds can be blown across fields, cross-pollinating other crops. Industrial farming waste pollutes communities and creates river and ocean dead zones.
4. Better taste, and richer in nutrients.
Researchers are slowly becoming aware that organics prove to be a much better option in nutritional vale and taste better too.
5. Eating with a sense of place.
Organic foods demonstrate a reverance for the land and it's people. Organics use less energy and their cultivation is beneficial to the soil, water, farmers and local inhabitants.
